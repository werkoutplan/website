Werkout Plan

Werkout Plan is a project that started publicly in January of 2018, and prior to that it was City Lights! since 2014. As an artist, Werkout Plan has always wanted to experiment with sounds and create things outside of the norm. He has played at many of the venues in the metro Detroit area including the Works, Elektricity, The Grasshopper, Marble Bar, and many more that are on that list. He currently has a single coming out January 11th of 2019 with Trashed Panda Music out of Detroit and a 4 track EP entitled “NIGHT_RIDE” coming not long after that. If you like heavy house beats, deep baselines, and catchy vocals, then Werkout Plan is the artist to look out for.
